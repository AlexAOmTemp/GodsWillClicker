# Description:

A clicker genre game.

The hero has an attack (button on the right) and 5 abilities (buttons on the left). Resources (at the top) allow using abilities more often and are dropped during combat.  
After a battle, you can choose a permanent bonus (increased health, damage, etc.).

**List of abilities, from top to bottom:**

1. **Armor** – blocks a certain amount of damage from the next hit.
2. **Flame Sword** – grants bonus damage that deals double damage to armor.
3. **Wings** – grant a chance to dodge an attack.
4. **Halo** – the next attack will be a critical hit. A critical attack deals half damage on a successful dodge.
5. **Divine Wrath** – ultimate ability, deals massive damage.
